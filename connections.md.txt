## Circuit Connections

North Direction:
Red LED    → Pin 2
Yellow LED → Pin 3
Green LED  → Pin 4

South Direction:
Red LED    → Pin 5
Yellow LED → Pin 6
Green LED  → Pin 7

All LED cathodes connected to GND using 220Ω resistors.


## Circuit Connections

| LED Color | Direction | Arduino Pin | Resistor |
|----------|----------|-------------|----------|
| Red      | North    | Pin 2       | 220Ω    |
| Yellow   | North    | Pin 3       | 220Ω    |
| Green    | North    | Pin 4       | 220Ω    |
| Red      | South    | Pin 5       | 220Ω    |
| Yellow   | South    | Pin 6       | 220Ω    |
| Green    | South    | Pin 7       | 220Ω    |

All LED cathodes are connected to GND through resistors.
